It's time to play with Chuck the Tumble Truck! You can do 4 different things in this simulation:
	1) Type 'B' to press Chuck's button.
	2) Type 'P' to push Chuck forward.
	3) Type 'T' to tickle Chuck.
	4) Type 'N' to leave Chuck where he is and do nothing.
Chuck wil respond depending on what you choose.
